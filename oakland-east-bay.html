Bay Area Fundamentals, East Bay Price Points

If you searched for a commercial real estate agent in Oakland, an East Bay CRE broker, or help to buy industrial, multifamily or retail property in the East Bay, I can help. The East Bay gives investors Bay Area demand drivers at generally lower entry prices than San Francisco or the Peninsula — which is exactly why so many of my out-of-state clients start here.

The I-880 corridor is one of Northern California's premier industrial and logistics markets; Oakland and Berkeley have deep multifamily stock; and the 680 corridor (Walnut Creek, Concord, San Ramon) anchors the suburban office market. I help you navigate all of it.
