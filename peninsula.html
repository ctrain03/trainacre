The Corridor Between San Francisco and Silicon Valley

If you searched for a commercial real estate agent in San Mateo, Redwood City or Palo Alto, or want to buy commercial property on the Peninsula, I can help. The Peninsula is a city-by-city market: Palo Alto and Menlo Park office trades on venture-capital adjacency, South San Francisco is a life-science hub, and Burlingame and San Mateo have some of the Bay Area's most durable downtown retail districts.

Inventory here is tight and often trades quietly. Having an agent who watches the corridor daily — and can move fast for a remote buyer — matters more on the Peninsula than almost anywhere else in Northern California.
