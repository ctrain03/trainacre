Frequently Asked Questions

Can you help me buy commercial property in California if I live in another state?

Yes — that's my core specialty. Most of my clients invest remotely. I handle tours (with video walkthroughs), local market intel, vendor and inspector coordination, and negotiation, so you can transact confidently without flying out for every step.

Which property types do you work with?

Office, retail, industrial/warehouse, multifamily (5+ units), mixed-use, and commercial land across Northern California.

Do you cover both the Bay Area and Sacramento?

Yes. I serve San Francisco, San Jose, Oakland, the Peninsula, and the Greater Sacramento region, including surrounding submarkets.

What does it cost to work with you as a buyer?

In most commercial transactions, buyer-side commission is paid from the transaction. We'll put everything in writing up front — no surprises.
